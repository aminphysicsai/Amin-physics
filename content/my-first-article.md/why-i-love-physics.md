Physics is not just a subject for me.
It is a way of seeing reality.

I love physics because it teaches me:
- How the universe works
- How logic defeats illusion
- How curiosity never dies

### Curiosity and Silence

Most answers in physics come from silence, patience,
and deep observation — not noise.

This is why physics feels like meditation to me.
